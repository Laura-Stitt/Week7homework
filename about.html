HaDerek Ministries exists to be an opportunity
for individuals, both Jewish and non-Jewish, to gather to worship
the G-d of Israel in The Way outlined in Torah. We beleve that salvation through faith as taught by Yeshua in the Messianic Writings, is not only compatible with, but also an integral part of a
Torah-observant lifestyle.
